Hyderabad
CHARMINAR
ANIMAL
Charminar is one of the oldest monuments in India, and it is situated in Hyderabad, Telangana. Charminar was built in 1591 and is a symbol of Hyderabad. This monument is the official emblem of Telangana. Charminar is derived from Urdu, meaning 'Four Pillars.
Hyderabad
GOLCONDA
FORT
Golconda was the principal capital of the Qutub Shahi kings. The inner fort contains ruins of palaces, mosques and a hill top pavilion, which rises about 130 meters high and gives a bird's eye view of other buildings. Golconda fort is undoubtedly one of most magnificent fortress complexes in India.
Hyderabad
MAKKAH
MASJID
The Mecca Masjid, located at a distance of about 200 metres from the famous Charminar, is the second largest mosque in India. The construction of the mosque began under Sultan Muhammad Qutb Shah, in 1614, but was only completed under Emperor Aurangzeb in 1694, after his conquest of Golconda.
Hyderabad
SALAR JUNG
MUSEUM
The museum has a rich library of rare books and illuminated manuscripts of enormous value. There are autographed manuscripts with seal and signatures of Emperors like Akbar, Aurangzeb and Jahanara Begum (daughter of Shah Jahan).
Hyderabad
RAMOJI
FILE CITY
Ramoji Film City is an integrated film studio facility located in Abdullahpurmet, Hyderabad, India. Spread over 1,666 acres, it is the largest film studio complex in the world and as such has been certified by the Guinness World Records.
Hyderabad
BIRLA
MANDIR
The temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu present in the form of Sri Venkateswara.The hillock is at a height of 280-feet situated on a 13-acre plot.The Birlas built the Hyderabad temple in 1976 and constructed it with white marbles imported from Rajasthan.
Hyderabad
FALAKNUMA
PALACE
Falaknuma Palace is Built in 1894, Taj Falaknuma Palace once served as home of The Nizam, a famed monarch. The palace is perched 2,000-feet above the historic royal city of Hyderabad, and features interiors and architecture reminiscent of the gilded age.
Hyderabad
HUSSAIN SAGAR
LAKE
Renowned as Asia's largest artificial lake, Hussain Sagar Lake is one of the most popular tourist attractions located in Hyderabad. The lake itself was commissioned by Ibrahim Quli Qutub Shah in 1563 and is renowned for its monolith of Lord Buddha that stands right in its centre.
Hyderabad
ZOO PARK
ANIMAL
The Zoo has certain specialties like Safari, Nocturnal Animal House, Reptile House, Birds of Prey enclosures, Butterfly Park, etc. some of which were launched first time in India.
Hyderabad
SANGHI
TEMPLE
Enchanting Sanghi Temple is located in Sanghi Nagar, 35 kms from Hyderabad in India. It is situated in a hillock called as Paramananda Giri, as one enters a huge Maha Dwaram or gateway welcomes the visitors. The Raja Gopuram can be seen from several kilometers away which is very tall.
